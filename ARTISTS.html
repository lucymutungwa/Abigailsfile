How They Got Famous

How They Got Famous

Discover how Tyler, the Creator, Travis Scott, Bryson Tiller, and Lil Tecca achieved fame in the music industry.

Tyler, the Creator

Tyler gained attention as the leader of the alternative hip-hop group Odd Future. His unique style and creative direction quickly set him apart, with solo albums like *Goblin* (2011) and *Flower Boy* (2017) propelling him to fame.

Travis Scott

Travis moved to Los Angeles to pursue music and gained traction after signing with Kanye West's GOOD Music and T.I.’s Grand Hustle label. His breakout mixtapes and the success of his album *Rodeo* (2015) helped launch his career.

Bryson Tiller

Bryson gained fame through SoundCloud, where his hit single "Don’t" went viral. His debut album *T R A P S O U L* (2015) further solidified his position in the R&B scene, earning him critical and commercial success.

Lil Tecca

Lil Tecca became famous with his breakout single "Ransom," which went viral on SoundCloud and TikTok. His youthful energy and catchy hooks resonated with fans, leading to the success of his debut project, *We Love You Tecca* (2019).

Tyler, the Creator on Loiter Squad

Tyler, the Creator on Loiter Squad

Explore Tyler’s Role and Characters on the Comedy Show *Loiter Squad*

About Loiter Squad

*Loiter Squad* was a sketch comedy television show that aired on Adult Swim from 2012 to 2014. Created by the collective Odd Future, the show was known for its outrageous comedy, skits, pranks, and unique characters. Tyler, the Creator, along with other Odd Future members, performed sketches, pranks, and skits that resonated with their fans and showcased their distinctive humor.

Tyler's Role on the Show

As a key member of Odd Future and one of the creators of *Loiter Squad*, Tyler, the Creator was a central figure in the show. Known for his versatile comedic talent, he played various characters in skits that were often wild, absurd, and over-the-top. Tyler’s unique sense of humor and personality were evident in many of the show’s most memorable sketches.

Notable Characters Played by Tyler, the Creator

  • Thurnis Haley - A hilarious golf enthusiast with a serious love for the game. Tyler’s portrayal of Thurnis, with his eccentric mannerisms, became one of the show’s most iconic characters.
  • Dr. Muhammed - A sketch where Tyler played a quirky doctor, poking fun at medical tropes and giving outrageous advice.
  • Young - A satirical character representing a stereotypical rapper, highlighting Tyler’s knack for comedy that combines social commentary with absurdity.
Travis Scott in Circus Maximus

Travis Scott in Circus Maximus

Exploring Travis Scott's Role in the Visual Project *Circus Maximus*

About *Circus Maximus*

*Circus Maximus* is a visual film project created by Travis Scott, released in conjunction with his 2023 album *UTOPIA*. The film is a unique blend of visual artistry and music, taking viewers on an immersive journey that mirrors the themes and sounds of *UTOPIA*. Directed by Travis Scott with the help of notable directors, the film pushes boundaries in music visuals.

Travis Scott's Role in *Circus Maximus*

Travis Scott not only stars in *Circus Maximus* but also serves as a visionary director. His goal was to create an experience that bridges the gap between music and cinema, giving fans an inside look into the world of *UTOPIA*. Throughout the film, Scott explores surreal and abstract visuals that reflect the intensity and range of his music.

Artistic Significance of *Circus Maximus*

With *Circus Maximus*, Travis Scott moves beyond traditional music videos, offering a cinematic experience that deepens the narrative of *UTOPIA*. This project highlights Scott's creative versatility, blending themes of fantasy, exploration, and introspection. The film contributes to the growing trend of visual albums and positions Travis as an artist who continually pushes creative boundaries.

Bryson Tiller's Role in Bad Boys for Life

Bryson Tiller's Contribution to Bad Boys for Life

Exploring Bryson Tiller's Role in the Soundtrack of *Bad Boys for Life* (2020)

About *Bad Boys for Life*

*Bad Boys for Life* is the third installment in the *Bad Boys* movie series, starring Will Smith and Martin Lawrence. Released in 2020, the film brought back the iconic duo of detectives for an action-packed storyline mixed with comedy and drama, receiving praise for its dynamic energy and thrilling sequences.

Bryson Tiller’s Role in the Soundtrack

Bryson Tiller contributed to the soundtrack of *Bad Boys for Life*, adding his smooth R&B sound to the film’s musical landscape. His track “Future Bright,” featuring Rick Ross, was included on the official soundtrack, complementing the movie’s themes of loyalty, resilience, and action. Tiller’s soulful style added a modern touch to the soundtrack, resonating with fans and contributing to the film’s atmosphere.

Impact on the Film’s Musical Atmosphere

With artists like Bryson Tiller on the soundtrack, *Bad Boys for Life* successfully fused R&B, hip-hop, and pop elements, aligning the music with the film’s energy. Tiller’s presence on the soundtrack elevated its emotional depth and provided a fresh, contemporary sound that appealed to a diverse audience. His contribution helped make the soundtrack a notable part of the movie's appeal.
